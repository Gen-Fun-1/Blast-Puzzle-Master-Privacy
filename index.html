Blast Puzzle Master — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07 December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Blast Puzzle Master (“we”, “our”, “the App”). This game respects your privacy and does not collect any personal information. The game works offline. The only time the App connects to the internet is to load advertisements.

2. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not collect:

  • Name, email, or phone number
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Contacts or messages
  • Precise location
  • Any personally identifiable information

3. Internet Usage

The game is offline and uses the internet only to display ads. No gameplay data or user data is uploaded or stored by us.

4. Advertising & Third-Party Data Collection

The App shows ads through third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob). These services may automatically collect:

  • Advertising ID or device ID
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (city-level)
  • Ad interactions (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected directly by the ad provider, not by us. For more details, please review Google’s Ad Policy:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data, since we do not collect user information. Any data used for ads is handled solely by third-party ad networks.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Security

Since we do not store personal information, there is no user data for us to protect. Third-party ad networks apply their own security policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Your continued use of the App after any update means that you accept the revised policy.
