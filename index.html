Blast Puzzle Master — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-07

This Privacy Policy explains how Blast Puzzle Master (“the App”, “we”, “our”) handles information. We respect your privacy. The game is designed for offline play and does not collect or store any personal data from users. The only time the App uses the internet is to load advertisements.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. We do not ask you to create an account or provide any personal details.

We do NOT collect:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Contacts or messages
  • Photos, media, or files from your device
  • Precise location data
  • Any personally identifiable information

2. Internet Usage

Blast Puzzle Master is an offline puzzle game. The App uses the internet only to request and show ads from third-party advertising networks. No other online features or cloud services are used by the App.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The App displays ads provided by third-party ad networks (for example, Google AdMob or similar services). These ad providers may automatically collect certain information for the purpose of serving and improving ads.

This information may include (depending on the provider and your device settings):

  • Advertising ID or device identifier
  • IP address and approximate location (e.g., city/region level)
  • App usage related to ads (such as ad impressions and clicks)

This data is collected and processed directly by the third-party ad providers, not by us. We do not have access to, store, or control this information.

For more details, please review the privacy policy of the ad provider you are using, for example:
Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Data Sharing

Since the App does not collect any personal data, we do not share user information with any third parties. Any data used for ads is handled directly by the third-party ad networks as described above.

5. Children’s Privacy

Blast Puzzle Master is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Ads shown in the App are subject to the ad network’s content policies and may be limited based on age-related settings or regulations where applicable.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store personal information on our own servers, there is no user data from the App for us to secure. Any data processed by third-party ad providers is managed under their own security measures and privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes means you accept the revised policy.
